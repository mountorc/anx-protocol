# Contributing Policy

This repository does not accept external contributions at this time.

## Policy Statement

The ANX Protocol project is maintained exclusively by the core development team. We appreciate your interest in contributing, but we have decided to keep development within our internal team to ensure consistency and quality control.

## Future Considerations

While we are not accepting external contributions currently, this policy may be revisited in the future as the project evolves.

## Contact

If you have questions or feedback about the project, please contact the maintainers through the project's issue tracker.

Thank you for your understanding.
